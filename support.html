FAQ

Does it work offline?

Yes, everything works offline. None of the features require internet.

The app uses a lot of storage. Why?

A lot of AI models were used in this app - LLM, TTS, Emotion Classifier. In addition to that, a few external libraries were used

Microphone isn’t detected

Go to System Settings → Privacy & Security → Microphone and ensure AI Companion is allowed. Restart the app after changing permissions.
