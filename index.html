FAQ

Does it work offline?
Yes, everything works offline. None of the features require internet.

The app uses a lot of storage. Why?
The app includes multiple AI models – LLM, TTS, and an Emotion Classifier. These require extra space.

Microphone isn’t detected
Go to System Settings → Privacy & Security → Microphone and allow AI Companion. Restart the app after changing permissions.

Privacy

Your words are yours. Everything you do in the app stays in the app.
